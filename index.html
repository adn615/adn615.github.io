About Me

Thoughtful design that blends function, calm, and personality.

As a senior interior design student on the cusp of graduation, I've developed a keen eye for creating spaces that blend aesthetic beauty with practical functionality. My passion lies in sustainable design approaches that respect both the environment and the end user's lifestyle.

Throughout my academic journey, I've had the opportunity to work on diverse projects ranging from residential renovations to commercial spaces. I believe that great design tells a story and creates meaningful connections between people and their environment.

When I'm not designing spaces, you can find me exploring design trends, drawing inspiration from nature, or working on personal creative projects.
